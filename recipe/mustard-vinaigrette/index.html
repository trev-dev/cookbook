Mustard Vinaigrette

  • Contributed By: Trev on May 21, 2022
  • Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/24609/mustard-vinaigrette/
  • prep: 5
  • cook: 0
  • ready: 5
  • servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon prepared Dijon-style mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 drops hot sauce

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the vinegar, honey, mustard, pepper, salt, garlic, oil and hot sauce until thoroughly combined. Chill until serving.
