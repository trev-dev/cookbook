Homemade Hoisin Sauce

  • Contributed By: Trev on Mar 2, 2022
  • Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/256166/homemade-hoisin-sauce/
  • prep: 10
  • cook: 0
  • ready: 10
  • servings: 1 1/2 cup

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup wheat free tamari
  • 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce, or to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk tamari, peanut butter, brown sugar, rice wine vinegar, sesame oil, hot sauce, garlic, and black pepper together in a large bowl.
